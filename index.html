Surfing in Canggu

Home to some of the most loved breaks in Bali and a mekka for seasoned surfers and beginners with plenty of homestays and affordable accommodations, understanding surfers’s needs. Canggu features several surf schools and also surf retreats, where you can sign up for all kinds of surf courses and adventures, often combined with some form of yoga and awesome community living.
